About Aliyu

Chappal Waddi — meaning "Mountain of Death" in Fulani — is Nigeria's highest peak at 2,419 metres. Located in Taraba State near the Cameroon border, within the Gashaka-Gumti National Park. The summit offers sweeping panoramic views of the Cameroon Highlands and the valley below. Rarely visited, almost never photographed. A true hidden gem.
